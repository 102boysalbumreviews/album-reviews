Review

The factual worst Bruno Mars album to date. With many of the 9 tracks featuring Carribbean-esque drumlines in the background that give the impression they were sampled straight from an elevator created in the 80s, Bruno's voice is left to carry the tracks. Lucky for Mars, that is what he is best at, salvaging a fair number of these songs to make them just relevant enough to tolerate listening. This album has a vast void of non-playlist tracks, which is very noncharacteristic of Mars and I, along with millions of other fans were left dissapointed with this February 27th new release. My personal favorites were On My Soul, Risk It All, and I Just Might. Album was reviewed on February 27th, 2026.

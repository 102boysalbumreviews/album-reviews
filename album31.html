Review

Chesle has been one of my quiet favorites for a few years at this point. Since the first of his songs I heard in 2022, he has gone from 14k to 104k monthly listeners on Spotify. I feel like part of my appreciation for this music comes from the fact that it feels like I was among the first to find him. Chesle boasts no formal album releases, only EPs and singles, with this project being his first EP since late 2025. In terms of the actual songs, they all have the same vibe that reminds me of teenage years in a small town. To me, the background tracks and tuned up vocals are comforting. The big differences in the song ratings mostly come from the vibe of the song since there is a certain style of his I like compared to others. My personal favorites were better me and talking to god.

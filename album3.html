Review

I thought to review this album after Zach and I had a week where we listened to a few of the songs and remembered how different of a time 2020 was before the COVID-19 pandemic. When this album dropped I was in my Spanish 4 classroom and not really a big fan at the time so I didn't understand the hype. Going back after hearing some of these songs countless times on TikTok, I have so much more respect of Uzi's fans and this album. As my first non-2026 release review, this was an amazing one to revisit. My personal favorites were Baby Pluto, Homecoming, and That Way. Album was reviewed on February 26th, 2026.

Review

My least favorite of the three albums released on the same day. Just an odd gray area around the genre, artist and vibe. Very glad these were all contained into the same album to make it easier to filter out and forget. Nothing blew me away on this album and it pales in comparison to the others, even with how mid those are. My personal favorites off this album were High Fives, WNBA, and Fortworth.

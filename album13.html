Review

The second movie playlist I have reviewed, another interesting collaboration for artists. Some tracks were made to feel like they were directly meant for a movie, without much utilization to be on a playlist outside that context. Overall, this movie is especially close to my heart because I saw it in theaters with my mom and sister and the ending brought them both to tears, which was a strong emotional response to the main themes of womanhood. In my opinion, having two songs from the same movie nominated for Oscars is legendary stuff and goes to show how this star-studded album and movie combination is an all time classic. My personal favorites off this album were What Was I Made For?, Dance The Night, and Silver Platter. Album was reviewed on March 26th, 2026.

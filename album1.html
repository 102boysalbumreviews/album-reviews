Review

Cloud 9 by Megan Moroney takes an upbeat approach to how relationships end. The overarching theme of this album as being the prize at the end of the day and maintaining self-worth even during the midst of some of the lowest points in their lives, Megan Moroney seeks to inspire women in similar positions to the ones she sings about in this 15-song masterclass. This was the first album I have reviewed after taking a long break and my, my, my was it one to remember for it's own reasons. My personal favorite tracks off this album were Wedding Dress, Medicine, and Stupid. Album was reviewed on February 24th, 2026.

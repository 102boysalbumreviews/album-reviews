Review

I feel guilty this is my third album in a row giving above 4 stars. There has been countless times I have listened to the whole album through on shuffle when I didn't know what else to listen to, so it was a fresh experience how the songs merged into one another in a set order. I don't have the exact numbers of how this stacks up to her other projects, but from what I remember, it is just about on par with the rest. I think the number of tracks heavily benefits the star rating in this case because none of the songs were too similar to get sick of in this quantity, but the choice of how many tracks to put on the album was for sure deliberate on Billie's/her label's behalf. I'm a huge fan of how her soft voice floats from chorus to chorus, and delicately dives into the themes around self perception. My personal favorite tracks were BIRDS OF A FEATHER, THE GREATEST, and DINER. Album was reviewed on March 26th, 2026.

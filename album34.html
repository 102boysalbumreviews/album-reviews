Review

I don't recall ever watching a movie or show starring Hilary Duff, but I do know she is one of those actress/singer hybrids. These were some of my first songs of hers I listened to, so in a way, I was going in blind. It's safe to say this was a good initial impression of her music. This soft voice, yearning vibe worked extremely well for a consistent feeling across the entire album. My personal favorites off this album were Holiday Party, You, From The Honeymoon, and Roommates.

Review

This album is connected to a lot of great memories, most of which revolve around the movie this dropped with and it being summer time. So many of these songs will make you feel some type of way. While there is such a variety of artists featured in these songs that don't necessarily have a whole lot in common with each other, Metro still compiles a cohesively themed soundtrack. While I think a downside that heavily affected the score was the fact that I'm not a big fan of some of the featured artists, it's all the more impressive that Metro Boomin acted as the bridge between them all. My personal favorites from this album were Self Love, Calling, and Am I Dreaming. Album was reviewed on March 12th, 2026.

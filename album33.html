Review

I've had my gripes with Future and his music, mostly because his mumble-rap style has never been in my heavily rotated genres. I will admit he does have some bangers here and there, which is the same for this album as well. I believe this album to be an experimental project from Future, trying out new things and trying to discover his love for the rap game over again. The simple album cover, title and vastly different sounds are among the most variety I've heard on an album this year. As such, the highs are high and the lows are very low. Across a few songs on this album, there is a very high pitched vocal track, most prominent on the song 2018, and totally flopped in my opinion. I also want to highlight how hype of a track Hollywood has, but I don't think it matches Future well and the vocals brought it all the way down to an A. The albums that scored the highest had the best overall mix of vocals and hip-hop beats. My personal favorites off this album were Alice, Fukk A Interview, and Money Over Everything. Album was reviewed on July 14th, 2026.

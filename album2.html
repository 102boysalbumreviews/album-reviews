Review

Classic Don Toliver putting his Donny T twist on the hip-hop game. A new take on many well-known samples such as in Sweet Home and Body. I had briefly skipped through a few of the song titles when assembling a running playlist and the overall result made me nervous about the quality of the full thing. I'm happy to say that many of these songs did not disappoint. My personal favorites off this album were Rosary, Gemstone, and E85. Album was reviewed on February 25th, 2026.

Review

Overall, I was unsurprised by how hot and cold this album is. It's the same story with every release he has. I think of him as a very acquired taste in music, dabbling in experimental tracks I don't necessarily enjoy. Many of these songs just lacked something, usually not the beat, since that what I have come to love Kanye for on all his hottest hits. It's like he knows what will be popular and enjoyable and he does the opposite. Even some of the features I will take the few hits we got out of him on this one for sure. My personal favorites off this album were FATHER, PREACHER MAN, and ALL THE LOVE. Album was reviewed on March 31st, 2026.

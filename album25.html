Review

This is the most "dancy" album of the 3 Drake released on the same day. I like to think the title is a hint at the fact that these should be on a wedding party playlist. Compared to the others, this one has the most features, samples of other songs, catchy hooks and best general quality. A few of these tracks are amazing in my opinion, but the average one is slightly above average and listenable. My personal favorites off this album were Hoe Phase, Which One, and Goose and The Juice.

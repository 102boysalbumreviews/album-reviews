Review

Sabrina Carpenter has been heavy in my rotation for a few years. I was so excited that she was dropping a new album, and it was on the last day of our trip to Disney, so there was nothing more I was looking forward to than this album. Safe to say it remains in the shadow of her other hits, with a few notable tunes coming out of this collection. The original version was released on August 29th, 2025, and this version added one additional song released on Christmas Eve, 2025. My personal favorites were My Man on Willpower, Goodbye, and Sugar Talking. Album was reviewed on March 12th, 2026.

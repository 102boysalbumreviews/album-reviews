Review

I don't have too many comments on this album. Just that is about average and right around where my expectations were set. I reviewed 3 albums on the same day and this was the one I put on for the longest just because I had no reason to look forward to it and wasn't seeing ANYTHING on social media about it. The typical song on this album was good, but I thought so so many of them just needed a touch "more". Something to hit the heartstrings, to stimulate the taps of feet along to the beat, or just something to stand out. I know this might come as a big surprise but I was catching a lot of shades of Harry Styles' albums released previously this year in Horan's album here. My personal favorites off this album were Monochromatic, Pretty, and Die If I Don't. Album was reviewed on June 16th, 2026.

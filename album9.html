Review

I just want to clarify that this is not my kind of music. I'm sure a fan of Carti would be more on board with his style, but to me, many of these songs sound the same, being carried by the beat, that after 34 songs, starts to morph into a general and bland track. Although there are some strong features on this album, they all play into Carti's style of vamp rage music and I am just not a big fan. I know Jake was excited for this one to drop on March 14th 2025, but to me, the deluxe version released 11 days after was a big part of it's saving grace. My personal favorites off this album were RATHER LIE, EVIL J0RDAN and LIKE WEEZY. Album was reviewed on March 17th, 2026.

Review

Peak Luke Combs. I love love love this album. I have always been a fan of his more upbeat songs because his strong voice belting out a chorus with a catchy punchline over a classic drum and guitar assembly is the recipe for a banger. Given that a few of these songs were released as singles, which I knew were all good, I was still surprised by the quality of the rest of the tracks. In my opinion this is such a good mix of themes around loving someone and the thoughts of losing someone you love. My personal favorite tracks were My Kinda Saturday Night, Alcohol of Fame, and Sleepless in a Hotel Room. Album was reviewed on March 24th, 2026.

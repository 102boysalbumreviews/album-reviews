Review

I fell in love with the energy of mgk's rock songs, most notably my ex's best friend and the entire lost americana album. This, is most definitely not that. I've come to realize how much of a hit or miss artist he is and it's safe to say, the album, was mainly a miss. A couple of factors like the shallow lyrics rapping about covering their bodies in ink and poor chemistry between the two title artists really stand out and make this album far below average. I don't have much else to say other than I was disappointed by this release, even with my low expectations on Wiz Khalifa. My personal favorites off this album were fill my pockets, girl next door, and passport. Album was reviewed on June 4th, 2026.

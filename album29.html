Review

This is just a continuation onto Olivia Rodrigo's past projects like SOUR and GUTS. This album is like a an athlete practicing their sport. Just polishing what made her past ones so amazing and even still improving. I do like how the first half of the album was all about the feelings of having a new crush, and the second half is how that develops into a realization that a relationship is toxic and is better to be over than endured. This sort of consistent emotion is exactly what makes listening to albums front to back so fascinating at the stories artists can tell over a 50 minute album, made of roughly 3 minute chapters as each song. Some of the tracks felt a bit too "out there" and repetitive, which is why this project is receiving such high praise, yet does not even have over 4 rating stars. My personal favorites off this album were my way, drop dead, and the cure. Album was reviewed on June 16th, 2026.

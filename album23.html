Review

The first of 3 Drake albums released on the same day that I reviewed. I don't find much repeatability in songs where Drake is just speaking lyrics, which is what much of this album is. That being said, there is some of the most creative lyricism in these tracks using double entendres galore floating from one verse to another. Overall pretty mid, but a few bangers in the mix. I think the quantity of tracks released across all albums watered down how good the best of the best are. My personal favorites off this album were 2 Hard 4 The Radio, Plot Twist, and What Did I Miss?. Album was reviewed on May 15th, 2026.

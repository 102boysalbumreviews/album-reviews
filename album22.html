Review

This album provides such a different listening experience to anything I've reviewed so far. The recording quality has the old time graininess that sounds like it is on an old radio microphone. That, paired with her accent and overall appearance paint the complete picture of the vibe this album gives off. Some of the lyrics are spoken like written poetry, and the echoes on many tracks give the effect of a spotlight on a gospel singer. There is so much history and nostalgia seemingly packed into this modern album that is honestly such an impressive feat. However, many of the songs are mid and not really something I would particularly add to a playlist. I enjoyed listening through it once, but I don't think I could do that again without getting bored. The spoken bits, and references to that fact that what she is saying is in a song, plus a whole song at the end dedicated to giving credit to people made it seem like this album was specifically meant to be reviewed as an album all the way through and that magic would be lost if I attempted again. My personal favorites off this album were Joy., WHERE IS MY HUSBAND!, and Skin & Bones.. Album was reviewed on April 29th, 2026.

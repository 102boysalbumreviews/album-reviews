Review

I like some EDM songs, but I would say they are generally not my favorite. I thought the tax-day release was a fun mixture of genres, languages, etc. I especially love the album theme of escaping the workplace and having new experiences after my girlfriend told me he is CPA certified and the album title and a few song titles are plays on words commonly found on in-office status updates. I could see some of these tracks absolutely hitting if I was surrounded by a crowd of people also into it, but during this first listen, the energy was not what I was looking for. My personal favorites from this album were LIGHTS GO OUT, SHADOWS, and MESS W/ ME. Album was reviewed on April 15th, 2026.
